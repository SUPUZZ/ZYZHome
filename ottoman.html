How to Style Your Storage Ottoman

A storage ottoman is one of the most versatile pieces of furniture you can add to your home. At 21 inches square, the ZYZ Home ottoman works beautifully as a coffee table alternative in compact living rooms. Top it with a decorative tray and a scented candle for an instant style upgrade.

In the bedroom, place it at the foot of your bed to store extra linens and provide a convenient seat while dressing. The flip-top lid keeps essentials neatly out of sight while remaining easily accessible.

Entryway Essential

Your entryway sets the tone for your entire home. Our houndstooth fabric ottoman offers a comfortable spot to put on shoes while the hidden compartment keeps scarves, gloves, and pet leashes organized. Available in Brown, Black, and Grey to match any color scheme.

Care & Maintenance

  • Vacuum fabric regularly with an upholstery attachment
  • Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth
  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fabric fading
  • Dust the solid wood panel with a soft, damp cloth
